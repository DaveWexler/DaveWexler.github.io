Science Fiction and Fantasy make the brain go 'round.

In the world of fiction, there are an infinite number of new worlds. Every time someone makes up a new rule of physics, a new realm of magic, or a new creature in our own world, an entire new universe is created. This new universe gives the author who made in a playground in which to experiment, create, and play with his or her characters, ideas, world dynamics...anything. And through experiencing these worlds of other people, we as readers or watchers expand our own minds a little, opening ourselves to the new possibilities of the fantastically creative. When you want a departure from our own world, there's no better place to turn.

The worlds I've created for my own work have ranged from the mystical to the modern, from the magical to the mundane. Some have magic, some have new imaginative technology, and some even have both. I invite you to come take a brief glimpse into the worlds I've created below, as categorized by the medium for which they've been written. Whatever you may think of them, I hope they at least give you that spark of a new world to explore.
